In closing, I must mention briefly the general lines of treatment. Having no specific treatment, we can but treat symptoms, and our efforts are directed mainly to supporting the strength of the patient. Hydrotherapy, quinin, iron, strychnia, salicylates, guaiaeol, mercurials, and various nuclein preparations are all used. Mention should also be made of the Credé ointment, the antistreptococcus serum, the bleeding and subsequent intravenous injection of salt solution, all of which at times seem to yield results.
